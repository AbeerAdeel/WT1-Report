Academic Studies

CIS 2750 (Software Systems and Development) provided a glimpse of Full Stack Development but with this work term I feel more confident and experienced with regards to Full Stack Development. Below are skills and aspects that I will take for the rest of my academic experience.

Agile Environment

Learning from an agile environment will help me collaborate with others in group related courses.

Databases

Focus of Work Term Report

I want to focus my work term report on the client transfer project I was assigned to with some other co-ops. The whole purpose of this project was that sales people can transfer their clients when they go on vacation. This was a tough project as there was a lot of edge cases and extreme scenarios to consider but this project overall made me a more versatile developer. Below are skills that I acquired throughout this assigned project.

TEAM COOPERATION

Having constant communication with the respective team and considering edge cases helped determine better design and coding practices.

SYSTEM ARCHITECTURE

With this project, it helped understand the full system architecure at Postmedia and will help adapt to new systems in the future.

UNIT TESTING

Writing unit tests with jest helped ensure the methodologies performed for the client transfer project were stable.

REST APIS

I gained a lot of experience with many different REST APIs as well as gained knowledge of when to use a specific REST API.







Conclusion

Overall, I was a Full Stack Developer at the Postmedia Innovation Outpost for 4 months and I learned exciting technologies such as React, Redux, MongoDB and TypeScript as well as participating in a live agile environment. This was my first team oriented work experience as my first co-op was an individual job and it definitely met my expectations. Many experiences that I learned from Postmedia Innovation Outpost, I plan on taking for the rest of co-op journey.
